Franz Tausch, Trois Duos pour Clarinette et Basson, Op. 21

Franz Tausch (1762–1817) was an early clarinet virtuoso active in Munich, Vienna (where he studied under Salieri), and Berlin. Among his students was Heinrich Baermann, whose son Carl is familiar is familiar to clarinetist for his Complete Method.
